## Problem
Buying groups coordinated orders over spreadsheets and chat, with no real platform behind them.

## Approach
A deployable-per-client web app: catalog, orders and back-office, themeable per brand, internationalized.

## Impact
Live in production for a real group, with a clickable demo. Built end-to-end as a reusable product.
