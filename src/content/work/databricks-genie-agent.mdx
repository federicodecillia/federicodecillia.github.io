## Problem
Retail teams waited on analysts for every data question. The bottleneck slowed assortment and space decisions across a global store network.

## Approach
As the team lead, I conceived and sponsored a Databricks Genie agent on top of the enterprise lakehouse, and led the team that built it. It is grounded on curated gold-layer tables in Unity Catalog (a medallion bronze/silver/gold architecture), so natural-language questions become governed, trustworthy queries. It goes beyond simple answers to advanced end-to-end analysis and visualizations: conversational analytics and business intelligence in one place.

## Impact
Adopted by the Retail Merchandising, Category Management, and Retail Space Management teams for day-to-day decisions, removing the analyst bottleneck and shifting them from reactive reporting to self-serve insight.

Built on Databricks Genie.
