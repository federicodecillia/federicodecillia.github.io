## Problem
Business teams waited on analysts for every data question. The bottleneck slowed assortment decisions across a global store network.

## Approach
I conceived and sponsored a Databricks Genie Agent on top of the enterprise data lake that turns natural-language questions into governed queries, and I contribute to its development.

## Impact
Now used by category teams for day-to-day decisions, removing the analyst bottleneck and shifting the team from reactive reporting to self-serve insight.

Built on Databricks Genie.
