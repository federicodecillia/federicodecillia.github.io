## Problem
Companies want chatbots that answer from their own knowledge, not generic models that hallucinate.

## Approach
I design and build retrieval-augmented agents that ground answers in a company's own documents and data, delivered through GPT Chatbot: vector search, grounded generation, and guardrails against hallucination.

## Impact
100+ chatbots shipped to production across businesses, handling real customer and internal queries.

Built with Claude and other LLMs.
