## Problem
Most organizations buy AI tools but adoption stalls: people don't know how to use them on real work.

## Approach
I run hands-on training and internal events: practical workshops on GenAI, LLMs and agents, tuned to each audience from beginners to technical teams.

## Impact
Teams move from curiosity to daily use, with concrete workflows they keep using after the session.
